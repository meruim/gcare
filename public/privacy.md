# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for QR Code-Enabled Appointment and Scheduling Manager Application (GCARE) - Guidance and Counseling Care Application

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to GCARE, the dedicated mobile application for the students of JRMSU Katipunan Campus. This Privacy Policy outlines how GCARE ("we," "our," or "us"), managed by the JRMSU Katipunan Campus Guidance Office, collects, uses, processes, and protects your information when you use our services. By utilizing GCARE, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently, recognizing the importance of guidance.

## Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect various types of information, falling into these categories:

### Personal Information (Information You Provide)

Student Account Data: When students register, we collect your Full Name, Student ID Number, Course, Block, Year Level, and a secure Password.

Counselor/Guidance Staff Account Data: For administrators, we collect your Full Name, Employee ID Number, Designation, and a secure Password.

Profile Information: You may optionally add other relevant details to your profile to assist in personalizing your experience or facilitating guidance interactions.

Appointment Details: Information related to appointments you schedule, including preferred time/date, purpose (e.g., academic, personal), and type of appointment.

Communication Data: Records of your interactions with us, such as support inquiries or feedback.

### Usage Data (About Your Interactions)

App Activity: Details of how you use the app, including features accessed, screens viewed, and time spent.

QR Code Interaction Data: Timestamps of QR code generations and scans, and the data embedded within them (e.g., appointment confirmations, attendance).

Notification Engagement: Whether you open or interact with notifications from GCARE.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Provision & Maintenance: To register and manage accounts, enable appointment scheduling, QR code generation, notifications, and ensure smooth app operation.

Improve User Experience: To understand usage, develop new features, enhance existing ones, and personalize content.

Communication: To send service announcements, security alerts, updates, and respond to your inquiries.

Analytics & Research: To understand user demographics, interests, and behavior within the JRMSU Katipunan Campus community.

Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, and investigate potential fraud, security breaches, and illegal activities, protecting the integrity of the Service.

Legal Compliance: To fulfill our legal, regulatory, or institutional obligations and enforce our terms.

## How We Share Your Information

We value your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing. We may share your information only under these limited circumstances:

With Other Authorized Users: For students, information embedded in QR codes you generate (e.g., Name, ID, Appointment Purpose) will be accessible to the designated Counselor or Guidance Office Staff who scans it for official purposes. For Counselors/Staff, relevant appointment management details are visible to authorized personnel.

With Your Explicit Consent: When you provide specific permission.

With Trusted Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) are granted access only as needed and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

For Institutional Changes: In events like a merger or transfer of assets, your information may be part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

For Legal Obligations & Protection: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of JRMSU Katipunan Campus, GCARE users, or the public.

## Data Security

We employ technical and organizational security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. You contribute to security by using strong, unique passwords.

## Your Data Protection Rights

As a user, you may have rights regarding your Personal Information, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing, and data portability. Where processing relies on consent, you have the right to withdraw it. To exercise these rights, please contact us. We will respond to legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe.

## Third-Party Services and Links

GCARE may contain links to external websites or services. These have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices. Please review their policies independently.

## Children's Privacy

GCARE is intended for JRMSU Katipunan Campus students, typically over 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data without consent, please contact us.

## International Data Transfers

Your information, including Personal Information, may be transferred to and processed in the Philippines. By using GCARE, you consent to such transfers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy here and updating the "Last Updated" date. We may also provide in-app or email notifications. Your continued use of the Service after changes indicates your acceptance.
