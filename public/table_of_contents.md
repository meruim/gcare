# GCare User Manual

---

## Introduction

GCare, or Guidance Care (QR Code-Enabled Appointment and Scheduling Manager Application), is an Android-based app designed for students of **Jose Rizal Memorial State University – Katipunan Campus (JRMSU-KC)**.

It allows students to quickly book appointments and view real-time schedules for various guidance services, promoting:

- Efficient use of time  
- Better academic support  
- Overall student well-being  

---

## Purpose of This Manual

The purpose of this manual is to guide students in using GCare efficiently and independently. It provides:

- Step-by-step instructions for scheduling appointments  
- Guidance on managing bookings  
- Tips for navigating the app with ease  

This manual equips students with the knowledge and confidence to access guidance services smoothly.

---

## System Requirements

- Device Compatibility: Android devices only  
- Operating System: Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher  
- Storage: Minimum free storage required  
- Internet Connection: Stable Wi-Fi or mobile data
