## Create Appointment Request

---

### 1. Click New Appointment

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Click <strong>New Appointment</strong> to start creating a new appointment request.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344700/kcbzpozhcf3pjwtqzfiv.webp" 
    alt="Click New Appointment"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 2. Select Category

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Select the <strong>Category</strong> that best matches your appointment. To cancel, click the <strong>X</strong> icon to exit.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344701/vbjzlie0knlwywv5unwb.webp" 
    alt="Select Category"
    class="w-full max-w-[220px] rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />

</div>

---

### 3. Fill in Required Details

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Enter all required information, such as <strong>Appointment Type</strong>, 
    <strong>Date & Time</strong>, and <strong>Description</strong>.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344705/evj5faxn0uu0zvgxu12v.webp" 
    alt="Fill Required Details"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 4. Select Appointment Type

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Choose the type of appointment you prefer with the counselor.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344704/t67kclfoyo32pkktzqe6.webp" 
    alt="Select Appointment Type"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 5. Choose Date and Time

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Select your preferred date and time for the appointment.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344708/f4s8tmug0so8hsaifphn.webp" 
    alt="Choose Date and Time"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 6. Add Description

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Add a short description or note for the counselor.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344707/zc69gg0gbzx7eecokmxf.webp" 
    alt="Add Description"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 7. Click Appoint Now

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Click <strong>Appoint Now</strong> to submit your appointment request.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344709/f4mrigyjggk2sxb9yypl.webp" 
    alt="Click Appoint Now"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 8. Wait for Staff Confirmation

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    Wait for staff confirmation. Your appointment may be <strong>approved</strong>, <strong>rescheduled</strong>, or <strong>cancelled</strong>.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772344712/mo7yglcqvccwpfxzricf.webp" 
    alt="Wait for Staff Confirmation"
    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>

---

### 9. Appointment Approved

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row items-center gap-6">
  <div class="flex-1 text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
    The <strong>Pending</strong> icon will change to <strong>Approved</strong> once the staff accepts your appointment. You will also receive a <strong>QR Code</strong>, which serves as a ticket when you go to the <strong>Guidance Office</strong>.
  </div>

  <img 
    src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dasd7oi7y/image/upload/v1772972584/nysjqhomulchkpnwxmyt.webp"

    class="w-[170px] h-auto rounded-2xl shadow-md"
  />
</div>
